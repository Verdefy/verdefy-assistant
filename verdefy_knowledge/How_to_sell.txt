How do I sell on Verdefy?

1. Visit https://verdefy.ng/closet-to-cash/
2. Click on Start Selling and create a seller account.
3. Click on GO TO DASHBOARD to sell directly or SETUP YOUR STORE to finish store setup.
4. Upload clear pictures of your clothing items.
5. Add title, size, condition, and Suggested price.
6. Submit for review. We approve listings in 1-2 hours.
7. And pick up your package on Tuesday or Friday 


Once approved, buyers will see your items on the site and can place orders. You'll get notified and paid after delivery.
